Keywords

  • Combustion: A chemical reaction between a fuel and oxygen. Makes heat, light and other product
  • Fuel: Any material that releases energy when burned
  • Vapour: Many small particles of liquid in the air
  • Ignition: Starting combustion

When something is burned, a chemical reaction occurs between the fuel and oxygen.

\[\begin{align*} & magnesium + oxygen \rightarrow \text{magnesium oxide} \\ & carbon + oxygen \rightarrow \text{carbon dioxide} \end{align*}\]

Fire Triangle

If all three sides of the triangle are present, a fire could start. By removing any of the three sides you can put out a fire.

How can you set paper on fire without destroying it?
